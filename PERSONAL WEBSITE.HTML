[Molemo Molaeeng Tlali]

Molemo is a dynamic and passionate media personality currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Broadcasting and Journalism at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology. With over a decade of experience across radio, television, and communications, she brings a strong foundation in both traditional and digital media. Her career has spanned several key roles—including news anchor, reporter, producer, and host—for leading broadcasters in Lesotho such as Lesotho National Broadcasting Services and Radio Lesotho. Beyond broadcasting, Molemo has also worked as a media liaison and event coordinator with the GEM Institute, and has contributed to administrative operations at Kopano Textiles. As a presenter on the Thahameso morning show, Molemo has delivered powerful national and international stories to the public. She has created impactful content, conducted meaningful interviews, and actively championed youth empowerment initiatives. Her dedication to excellence and innovation has earned her recognition from Setsoto FM and UNESCO’s Lesotho Youth Desk, celebrating her work in media presentation and youth-focused storytelling.
